Biography

Mathieu Lemire was born in 1988 Montreal,QC Canada. Lemire learned the basics of photography at the age of 12 and he got his first camara in 2007, a dslr 30D.

For him, photography is freezing moments that take place in a fraction of a second, capturing the best angle and lighting when it's all at the right place!

Inspired by:

Annie Leibovitz, Cole Barash, Chase Jarvis, Erik Seo.
